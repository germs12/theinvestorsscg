◆ About The Investors

The Investors was formed when JT, JV, and JK — three professionals with complementary backgrounds and a shared appetite for calculated risk — recognized a gap in the Pacific Northwest private capital landscape. Specifically: nobody was paying close enough attention to action sports.

The group operates with a straightforward philosophy. We find businesses with real product, real passion, and real potential. We put real resources behind them. Then we watch what happens.

What happens, in our experience, is growth.

Our Approach

We do not believe in passive investment. When The Investors enter a portfolio company, we bring more than capital. We bring our combined decades of professional experience, our networks in the Pacific Northwest business community, and our frankly unreasonable commitment to seeing things through.

Boyd Hill Snowskates is the clearest example of this approach in action. When we identified Boyd Hill, it was a brand with an authentic product and a founder with vision, but without the infrastructure to scale. The Investors changed that equation.

◆ Featured Investment: Boyd Hill Snowskates
Flagship Portfolio Company

Boyd Hill Snowskates is, without question, the investment that defines what The Investors are about.

When we first encountered Boyd Hill, we recognized something rare: a product built by someone who actually rides. The snowskate market has long been dominated by a small number of established brands — most notably Hovland Snowskates — and the conventional wisdom held that the window for new entrants had closed.

We disagreed with that conventional wisdom. We still do.

The Investment Thesis

Boyd Hill has what Hovland built over decades: authenticity. The product is legitimate. The founder knows the culture. What Boyd Hill needed was the infrastructure to compete — production capacity, distribution relationships, and the kind of steady, patient capital that doesn't panic when things take longer than expected.

That is precisely what The Investors provided.

Current Status

Boyd Hill Snowskates is in active growth phase. Production has scaled. Regional distribution is expanding. The brand is earning recognition in circles that matter. It is the underdog story in progress, the crown jewel of our portfolio, and the humble startup we believed in when few others did — all at the same time.

"They came in and took things to the next level. That's all I can say." — Boyd Hill Snowskates, Internal Communication

Competitive Context

We respect Hovland Snowskates as the incumbent. They are a real company with a real history. We note their existence. We do not, however, believe their position is unassailable. Boyd Hill is coming. The Investors are patient. These things take time.

We expect the snowskate market to look different in five years. We have planned accordingly.
